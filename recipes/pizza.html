This Homemade Pepperoni Pizza has everything you wantâ€”a great crust, gooey cheese, and tons of pepperoni. The secret to great pepperoni flavor? Hide extra under the cheese! Who needs delivery?

Ingredient's

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8oz of fresh mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup of sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 basil leaves
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil (for drizzling)

Method

  • Pre heat oven to 220c
  • Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough.
  • Top with fresh mozzarella and tomatoes and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  • Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of rep pepper flakes.
  • Drizzle with olive oil to serve.
